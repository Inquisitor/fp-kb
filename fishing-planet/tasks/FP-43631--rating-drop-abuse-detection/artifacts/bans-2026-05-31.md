## Trajectory analysis (Mongo Tournament-log)

All five exhibit the **same rhythm**:

1. **Batch no-show flushes at identical timestamps** тАФ player went offline for hours or days,
   queued registrations accumulated, all penalties flush in one second when they log back in.
   This is the cleanest behavioural signature: not "couldn't make it" by accident, but
   pre-registered systematically without showing up.
2. **Strong positive on PLAYED entries** тАФ podium-win deltas of +25 to +55 are common. These
   are skilled players (TOPS-caliber); their natural PCR would be much higher.
3. **PCR oscillates 0тАУ230** тАФ repeatedly hits the floor of the NOOBS bracket (`[0тАУ100]`),
   never approaches TOPS (`1001+`). The "tank тЖТ climb тЖТ tank" cycle keeps registration in
   NOOBS / low-MIDDLES, which is where the easy prizes are.
4. **No fish / water / lure preference** for which tournaments they play vs skip тАФ selection
   is by *presence* (logged in or not), not by tournament theme. So tanking is opportunistic
   offline behavior, not pre-meditated per-tournament targeting.
