## Verdict legend

Verdict in the TSV is reduced to two values to give a clean picture for Support:

- **`BANNED`** — comp-banned by us this cycle. Profile + LB + Mongo banLog flipped; `BanEnd` is
  2026-06-22 (NEW, 2W) or 2026-07-06 (REPEAT, 4W). Support decides durable-ban duration on top.
- **`WATCH`** — we reviewed and chose not to ban. Three reasons (see Reading notes per row):
  1. MIDDLES/TOPS-only veteran flavor — pattern is the opposite of NOOBS-farming
  2. Borderline at the gate thresholds (no-show share ~30–34%, may be casual misses)
  3. Adversarial-review downgrade — pattern present but sample too thin for first-time conviction

`IsBanned=true` on a WATCH row is real DB data — usually an old expired ban (e.g.
`yaobaizhishang` had a 2024 ban that predates the matchmaking system entirely) — not our action.

> Per-bracket cells use `N / M / T` **with spaces** on purpose — `8/12/0` gets auto-parsed as a date by Google Sheets.
